Subtraction becomes easier when you remove only what the question asks for and then pause.

## Example: 8 - 2

1. Start from **8**.
2. Remove two lower beads.
3. Read again: **6**.

## Practice habit

Say the starting number, the change, and the final number in one calm sentence.
