This lesson is about staying organized, not being fast.

## Example: 24 + 7 + 1

1. Start from **24**.
2. Add **7** carefully to reach **31**.
3. Add **1** to reach **32**.
4. Read the whole number after each move.

## Practice habit

Pause between steps and confirm the tens and ones rods separately.
