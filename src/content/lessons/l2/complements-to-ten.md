Complements to ten matter when a digit needs help from the next place or from a cleaner mental pattern.

## Pairs to remember

- 1 and 9
- 2 and 8
- 3 and 7
- 4 and 6
- 5 and 5

## Step by step for adding 3 to 7

1. Start from **7**.
2. Notice that **7 needs 3 more to reach 10**.
3. Complete the ten.
4. Re-read the value before continuing.
