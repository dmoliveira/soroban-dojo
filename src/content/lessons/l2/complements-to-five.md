Complements help when the direct movement is clumsy. Instead of forcing every bead one by one, think in number pairs.

## Pairs to remember

- 1 and 4
- 2 and 3

## Step by step for adding 3 to 2

1. Start from **2**.
2. Notice that **2 needs 3 more to reach 5**.
3. Move to the clean five shape.
4. Read the result: **5**.
